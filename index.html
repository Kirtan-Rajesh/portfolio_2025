About Me

Hey there — I'm Kirtan, an AI and Robotics Engineer who's endlessly curious about how machines think, move, and sometimes break (that’s where the real learning happens). I believe curiosity isn't just a trait — it's my most powerful tool.

My work lives at the intersection of AI models and robot wheels — building smart, adaptive systems that turn code into real-world magic. From AI-powered applications to autonomous robots, I love crafting solutions that blur the line between digital intelligence and physical interaction.

I'm also big on research — not the stuck-in-a-library kind, but the let’s-experiment-and-break-things kind. I thrive on learning new ideas and figuring out how we can turn them into practical, real-world systems.
